About me

Hi, I'm Kathy! I'm a San Francisco-based business strategist, designer, and artist. I love helping companies think through new product and business ideas - and then diving into the hands-on work to actualize those visions.

I've worn (and wear) many hats, because I believe that innovation often comes from exploring the intersections between disciplines, industries, and cultures. Currently, I work on the strategy team at Credit Karma, where I evaluate and prioritize new growth opportunities for the company. Before that, I worked on design thinking / customer strategy projects at Deloitte and honed my skills in user research. I also studied the social sciences at Princeton, which has continued to influence my approach to UX research as well as my beliefs around the problems that are most pressing to solve.

Outside of my career in tech, I'm passionate about making art. I truly believe we could all use more beauty in our lives and that it is part of what makes us human.

I'm always eager to chat about opportunities to work together, or to just exchange stories over coffee. Feel free to get in touch!
