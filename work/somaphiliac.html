Somaphiliac logo

Logo design for skincare & clean beauty blog • 2018

Somaphiliac is a blog about skin care and clean beauty. I worked on a new logo to convey the site's commitment to honest beauty products that are kind to our bodies and our earth.

Role
Logo design

Final work →

As I set off to design Somaphiliac's logo, I knew it was important that what we came up with would be a visual extension of the blog's broader identity. Somaphiliac's creator wanted the site to represent simplicity, honesty, and respect - treating your body well, going back to basics, and making conscious decisions.

Based on this vision, I sketched out a couple dozen ideas, drawing on motifs related to nature, skin, and down-to-earth luxury. We settled on a lettermark logo that wove in the concept of layers (to evoke skin and smoothness) and leaves (to represent nature and freshness).

The icon is now in use both on Somaphiliac's website and social channels.
